According to Bizideas you are:
The visionary


You are a person with a strong purpose in life and feel that you need to make an impact. You are interested in solving big problems facing the human race and the Earth and making our world a better place to live. Why not explore life on other planets. You are an innovator and should follow a leadership strategy. You can tolerate risk in doing your endeavours. Some of which have not been done up to now. You want to develop products and services that the world has not seen up to now and bring it to the larger public. And you want to make money while doing so. You are willing to run a company that is growing, attracts funding from investors and scales rapidly. You have the capacity not only to manage people, but also to lead them in your visionary direction.

The ideas to develop need to solve big problems in your city, your country, the world. Take a look around. Observe mindfully and see what are the structural issues that the communities are tackling. Read the news, read reports, read recommendations by big organizations and choose the one that brings passion and motivation to solve. Talk to experts in the field, collect as much information as you can and bring a team to brainstorm on possible solutions. You are a leader and innovator and the business idea will eventually come from you and your team. Possible problems that you can tackle are the pollution, the urbanisation, the deforestation, famine, etc.

For motivation, follow SpaceX , Apple, Google, Facebook, etc. and the Most innovative growth companies list.
The builder of tomorrow

You are a person who is truly interested in how things function and operate. You enjoy decomposing different stuff into its building blocks. But mostly, you like envisioning and then assembling or creating useful results of your work. You are a creator and should follow a leadership strategy. You can tolerate relative risk in doing your endeavours. You really want to take your time and work in solitude, but the people that are looking up to you need your guidance and support. And you want to make money while doing so. You are willing to run your personal practice or company that is profitable and earns you more money than the average person in your circle of people.

You are a problem solver by heart, so in order to come up with your idea start observing your reality. Do you struggle with a task at hand? Are the processes working well? Is there a new problem that needs to be solved in your industry or profession? Is there anything that frustrates you? Can you come up with an improved way of doing things? You really need to pay attention and take note of what is not working and can be done better or in a completely new way. Possible industries that you can explore are ICT, Electronics, Transport and Logistics, Telecommunications, Engineering, Physics, etc.

For motivation, follow the new trends for companies every year, for example Forbes list on the next billion-dollar startups.
The money making optimist


You are a person who is interested in excellence, doing things exceptionally well and with the right attitude. You have business instincts and/or education and are aspiring to be a financially well-rounded person, which is also valid for your business ventures. You are engaging, motivating, optimistic, with a positive, growth mindset. You can somehow thrive through higher risk situations by your ability to analyse the situation and find the best outcomes. You know how to sell and market a good product or service. You are happy when you can find a good balance between your work/business and family, friends, social life. You have good people skills and know how to use the right rhetoric to achieve your goals.

You are a problem solver by heart, so in order to come up with your idea start observing your reality. Do you struggle with a task at hand? Are the processes working well? Is there a new problem that needs to be solved in your industry or profession? Is there anything that frustrates you? Can you come up with an improved way of doing things? You really need to pay attention and take note of what is not working and can be done better or in a completely new way. Possible industries that you can explore are Finance, Accounting, HR, Marketing, Services, etc.

For motivation, follow the new trends for companies every year, for example Forbes list on the next billion-dollar startups.
The small business owner


You are a person who is interested in doing business probably for a while. You have experience in different fields as an expert and now you want to launch your own venture. You have business instincts and know that you can do better than some existing companies. You are hard-working, resourceful and a person who achieves their goals. You can somehow thrive through moderate-risk situations by your ability to analyse the situation and find the best solution. You are happy when you can find a good balance between your work/business and family, friends, social life. Why not even work with them. You have good management skills and know how to get out of any situation.

You are a problem solver by heart, so in order to come up with your idea start observing your reality. Do you struggle with a task at hand? Are the processes working well? Is there a new problem that needs to be solved in your industry or profession? Is there anything that frustrates you? Can you come up with an improved way of doing things? You really need to pay attention and take note of what is not working and can be done better or in a completely new way. Possible industries that you can explore are Trade, Online trade, Tourism, Food and Drinks, Agriculture, etc.

For motivation follow the list of the most thriving companies and copy and improve their strategy and business models, like the Capital 100 - The biggest companies in BG.
The artistic entrepreneur


You are a person who is interested in creativity, design and everything artistic and you are also aware that you want to make a business out of it. You have experience in different fields as an expert/artist/craftsman or woman and now you want to launch your own venture. You have the attitude of a creator. You are motivated to start something from scratch and make it a masterpiece. You don’t tolerate high risks, but you can always find a business partner to provide that mindset and agility. You love working on your own or in a smaller team and know well a certain target audience. Probably you yourself are part of your target market. You know well what are the tastes in your field. So follow your heart, research the tendencies and pick one to start with. Possible industries that you can explore are Arts, Architecture, Urbanism, Design, etc.

For motivation go to Etsy and browse your area of interest.
